About

Excellence is not just a goal for me; it is a standard. I approach every project with intellectual curiosity, technical rigor, and a drive to produce work that is both functional and exceptional. Whether I am building scalable ML models, engineering data pipelines, or communicating insights through compelling visualizations, I am committed to delivering results that truly stand out.
